Values and strengths

Values:

Expressing myself
my thoughts and feelings. This helps me process thoughts and processes externally. I do this through conversation, art, writing and collecting.
Humour
Humour is the best way I express myself and connect with others. Laughing with people is the best way to connect and feel bonded
Friendship and Empathy
According to friends: “you make a considered effort to make sure people remain in your life. And the empathy goes both ways like you’re a shoulder and your friends can do this for you — condition of a friend is this shared empathy”

Strengths and Limitations:

One biggest strength is that I am a hard worker. I give a job 100% of my effort and really get stuck into tasks or jobs. Unfortunately there is a down side that is I won’t do as well if I don’t enjoy it. This is why I am doing dev as I do enjoy it and want a career that I enjoy doing.

My biggest limitation is my self-criticism. I have always been overly self critical and it has never gotten me anywhere. It is only when I let go of my too-high expectations that I do well and enjoy myself in the process.
