PROBLEM SOLVING

The problem and Solution:

I ran into a lot of problems doing the kata’s as I felt ill prepared in my javascript knowledge in comparison to what was being asked.

I asked my peers, mostly at the start to help me get over a few hurdles. Towards the end I managed to figure out what was being asked and was able to simply google it from there.

Reflection:

I felt extremely overwhelmed and terrible. There was a huge gap between starting and finishing as my anxiety started to escalate. Sometimes when something is making you anxious, the thing that is causing this manifests into a much bigger problem - eventually becoming a trigger. It also hinders your normal brain function where communication becomes really difficult and ones problem solving ability becomes drowned out by the cycle of anxious thinking.

I learned a couple of interesting things. The first being when I am in a state of anxiety one thing I need is to be listened too. I didn’t realise how much I needed this until it happened. I found myself calm down after feeling as though a) my worries were being heard and b) they were being addressed.
The second thing I am completely capable. After I had calmed down I was able to do it significantly better than before which really highlighted how debilitating my mindset made me.
